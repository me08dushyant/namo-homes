Namo homes is a unique concept of 212 ultra-luxurious Villaments, where you get the luxury and comfort of a villa along with amenities of an apartment.

Sprawled over 6.5 acres on Shivpuri-Gwalior National Highway (NH 27), Jhansi, the beautifully designed duplex villaments, offer all contemporary world-class facilities. The thoughtful design offers unobstructed views, spacious double-height living rooms, natural light at every level and complete privacy.
